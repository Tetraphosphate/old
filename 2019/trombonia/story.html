Sad Trombone

Real Name: Bolero

Nickname Origin: He has a habit of saying "wah wah wah wahhhh" whenever someone fails.

Type: F-Attachment Tenor Trombone

Likes: Other people's failures, glissandos, complaining

Dislikes: Failing, people who complain, hypocrites

French Horn

Real Name: No one knows.

Nickname Origin: No one knows what his real name is, and he's never said, so they just call him French Horn, or just Horn.

Likes: Going on holiday, having fun, trying new things

Dislikes: Uptight people, people who don't want to have fun

Somewhere on a Planet, deep in intergalactic space:

"Sackbuts are better than trombones" - The Sackbut

"Hey, trombones, trumpet, euphoniums, and tuba, listen up for a sec..." - Flugelhorn

"What about the baritones?" - Baritone Horn

"Aren't they the same as euphoniums?" - Interrupted Flugelhorn

"No, no! How many times do I have to tell you, baritones are not euphoniums!" - Annoyed Baritone

"Of course not! Euphoniums sound much better, which is why they're named after the Greek word for nice-sounding, euphonos." - Nice-Sounding Euphonium

"Y'all are just tuba impersonators! Tuba's obviously the best, and the originalest" - Tuba

"And before you say it - I know originalest isn't a word. Don't tell me." - Tuba, again

Saxhorn joined the conversation.

"*ehm*. Just fyi, tubas, euphoniums and baritones are all inspired by the saxhorn. So who's the impersonators now, huh?" - Saxhorn

"Wah wah wah wahhhh" - Sad Trombone

Saxhorn left the conversation.

"Sheesh. That was unnecessary." - Tuba

Saxhorn yeeted off the planet.

"HeY, gUyS. lIsTeN!!! Yesterday, I had the idea to start a band..." - Interrupted Flugelhorn

"Dibs first trumpet part!" - Trumpet

"You're the only trumpet, man" - Baritone

French Horn joined the conversation.

"D'y'all know, French horns aren't actually French? They're from somewhere around Germany, first used as hunting horns." - French Horn

"Also, did someone say band? I'm in!" - Horn

"Alright, good! First band practice is tomorrow, same time, same place, bring your friends." - Flugelhorn

At Tenor's house, on that same Planet:

There's a trombone family reuinion, all the trombones are there, including the sackbuts.

Tenor: "Hey guys, I'm so glad you all actually turned up! I've..."

Bass Trombone (Hortensius): "It's not like we had anything else to do"

Tenor Trombone: "So what've you planned? Are we just gonna sit here for two hours?"

Alto Trombone (Allora): "Shut up! Let Tenor explain..."

*Tenor clears his non-existant throat.*

Tenor: "So, as I was going to say,"

*He glares at Hortensius and TeNoR tRoMbOnE*

Tenor: "I was thinking we should chat a bit and get to know each other better, as we'll be working together to upstage the rest of the instruments in band quite a lot, as trombones."

Tenor: "I guess I'll start. I'm Tenor, you guys know that."

Sad Trombone: "I'm Bolero, but y'all can call me Sad Trombone. And as the only tenor-bass trombone here, I'm obviously superior. Hey, I was just kidding, don't look at me like that."

Bass Trombone (Hortensius): "Y'all know who I am and I know who y'all are. This is pointless."

*Hortensius gets up and leaves.*

Tenor Trombone: "I'll be going too."

Allora, as her brother Tenor Trombone leaves: "I'll have a talk to you later!"

Sackbut, to Tenor: "Don't mind them. We don't need them."
