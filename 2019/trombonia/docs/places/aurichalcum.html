Aurichalcum

Details

Description: Aurichalcum is a moon that orbits the Lonely Runaway Planet, along with Aeramentum. It has this name, as it resembles a speck of yellow brass in the sky (or so the trombones say). The yellow colouring is caused by iron sulfide in the moon's crust. Aurichalcum is roughly twice the size of Aeramentum, though they are both asteroids captured by the Lonely Runaway Planet as it moved through its orbit.

Inhabitants: Space Trombone
