Aeramentum

Details

Description: Aeramentum is, along with Aurichalcum, a moon that orbits the Lonely Runaway Planet. It's red colour is due to the high concentration of iron oxide in its crust. The instruments living on the planet below named it Aeramentum as it looked like a grain of shining red brass in the sky (that's what they said, anyway). Aeramentum is about half the size of Aurichalcum, and they are both asteroids captured by the Lonely Runaway Planet as it moved through its orbit.

Inhabitants: None
