The Triumvirs

Details

Members: Hortensius, Catulus, Lucius

Description: Hortensius, the founder of the not-cult ("It's not a cult!" - Hortensius), named their friend group the Triumvirs (nothing to do with the ancient Roman Triumvirates), as there are three of them. Their rivals are the "Trisense" - Lucius claims they copied their name from the Triumvirs, they often compete for the trombone parts in band, and they just generally dislike each other. They often meet up in Lucius' house to discuss their plans to take over the Lonely Runaway Planet, annoy the Trisense, and to hang out.
