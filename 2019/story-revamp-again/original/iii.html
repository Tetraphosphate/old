Somewhere on a Planet, deep in intergalactic space:

"Sackbuts are better than trombones" - The Sackbut

"Hey, trombones, trumpet, euphoniums, and tuba, listen up for a sec..." - Flugelhorn

"What about the baritones?" - Baritone Horn

"Aren't they the same as euphoniums?" - Interrupted Flugelhorn

"No, no! How many times do I have to tell you, baritones are not euphoniums!" - Annoyed Baritone

"Of course not! Euphoniums sound much better, which is why they're named after the Greek word for nice-sounding, euphonos." - Nice-Sounding Euphonium

"Y'all are just tuba impersonators! Tuba's obviously the best, and the originalest" - Tuba

"And before you say it - I know originalest isn't a word. Don't tell me." - Tuba, again

Saxhorn joined the conversation.

"*ehm*. Just fyi, tubas, euphoniums and baritones are all inspired by the saxhorn. So who's the impersonators now, huh?" - Saxhorn

"Wah wah wah wahhhh" - Sad Trombone

Saxhorn left the conversation.

"Sheesh. That was unnecessary." - Tuba

Saxhorn yeeted off the planet.

"HeY, gUyS. lIsTeN!!! Yesterday, I had the idea to start a band..." - Interrupted Flugelhorn

"Dibs first trumpet part!" - Trumpet

"You're the only trumpet, man" - Baritone

French Horn joined the conversation.

"D'y'all know, French horns aren't actually French? They're from somewhere around Germany, first used as hunting horns." - French Horn

"Also, did someone say band? I'm in!" - Horn

"Alright, good! First band practice is tomorrow, same time, same place." - Flugelhorn
