F-Attachment Tenor Trombone





"Let's go camping!" - Flute

"YaY! i LoVe CaMpInG! cAmPiNg Is FuN!" - TeNoR tRoMbOnE

"Awwww... Why?" - Rest of band moans

"Rule #3: Band camp is compulsary." - Interrupted Flugelhorn, reading from his book, 101 Band Rules

"So... List of things we need:

  • Tent
  • Gas Cooker
  • Food
  • I really can't think of anything else..." - Flute

*Rest of band packs their camping gear*

"We'll tramp there, since it's only a 50km walk." - Flute

*Bass Trombone groans inwardly*

"YaY! i LoVe GoInG fOr 50Km WaLkS!" - TeNoR tRoMbOnE

"Can't you just shut up? I'll give you a lolly if you do." - Egoistic Trumpet

"Rule #13: Do NOT give lollies to TeNoR tRoMbOnE." - Interrupted Flugelhorn, reading from his book, 101 Band Rules

"Somebody burn his book of rules. Please. It'll be better for all of us." - Bass Trombone mutters to Euphonium

Ten Hours Later...

"Halfway there, guys! You're doing great!" - Flute

"Why didn't we just catch the bus? We're only going to the park (the 50km away one)." - Bass Trombone says to Euphonium

"Such a waste of time. As if going to camp wasn't enough." - Bass Trombone is ReAlLy AnNoYeD

"Can't you just shut up? I'll give you a lolly if you do." - Egoistic Trumpet

*Bass Trombone throws a rock at Egoistic Trumpet*

"HEY! Not nice! Rule #12: Do NOT throw rocks at others. Say sorry to Egoistic Trumpet. NOW!" - Interrupted Flugelhorn

"SoRrY, eGoIsTiC tRuMpEt, FoR tHrOwInG a RoCk At YoU" - Bass Trombone

"You just broke Rule #30: Do NoT sPeAk LiKe ThIs To FlUgElHoRn. Even if your name is TeNoR tRoMbOnE." - Interrupted Flugelhorn

*Bass Trombone groans inwardly for the 1000th time*

Another Ten Hours Later... It is now night.

*Flugelhorn pitches a tent*

"Ouch!" - Tent Pole whacks Flugelhorn in the face

"Double Ouch!" - Tent Pole whacks Flugelhorn in the face again

*Tenor gives Flugelhorn a hand at pitching the tent*

"Done!" - Flugelhorn and Tenor

*Everybody piles into the tent and sleeps*
