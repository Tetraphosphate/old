101 Band Rules

By the Interrupted Flugelhorn

  1. You may NOT eat during Band Practice.
  2. If your name is TeNoR tRoMbOnE, you are ONLY allowed lollies twice a week, on Wednesdays and Saturdays.
  3. Band Camp is compulsary.
  4. Do NOT leave instruments on the floor where they can be stood on (i.e. don't lie on the floor).
  5. Be nice to others.
  6. Be nice to Flugelhorn especially.
  7. Do NOT shout.
  8. Do NOT cause others pain.
  9. Do NOT burn your sheet music, even if you hate it.
  10. Say no rude words.
  11. Play at the written tempo and dynamic range.
  12. Do NOT throw rocks at others.
  13. Do NOT give lollies to TeNoR tRoMbOnE.
  14. Do NOT make fun of others.
  15. Do NOT boast too much about your achievements. Others may not necessarily want to listen to your Incredible List Of Achievements.
  16. Practice at least 14 times a week.
  17. Do NOT play wrong notes.
  18. All concerts are compulsary.
  19. Do NOT kill others if they score better than you in auditions.
  20. Do NOT play ear-piercing high notes, unless they are written in your music.
  21. Practice if you have spare time.
  22. There Is No Such Thing As Too Much Practice.
  23. Practice until you can't get it wrong.
  24. Do NOT empty spit valves on the carpet.
  25. Do NOT empty spit valves on others.
  26. Do NOT empty spit valves on other's belongings.
  27. The best place to empty spit valves is outside.
  28. If you have spare time, PRACTICE!
  29. As mentioned before, There Is No Such Thing As Too Much Practice.
  30. Do NoT sPeAk LiKe ThIs To FlUgElHoRn. Even if your name is TeNoR tRoMbOnE.
  31. If you do, you shall spend 5 minutes outside in the rain.
  32. There is a Rain Simulator (i.e. the outdoor shower) outside if it is a sunny day.
  33. Do NOT eat in the practice room.
  34. The practice room is for Practice, not for games.
  35. You should not be playing games when you have music to practice.
  36. Band Hierarchy (from most important to least important):
  37. Flugelhorn
  38. Flute
  39. Rest of band
  40. Tuba
  41. Parts:
  42. Soprano Cornet: Egotistic Trumpet
  43. All Cornet Parts (Solo, Repiano, 2nd, 3rd): Egotistic Trumpet
  44. Flugelhorn: Interrupted Flugelhorn
  45. All Tenor Horn Parts (Solo, 1st, 2nd): TeNoR tRoMbOnE
  46. All Baritone Horn Parts (1st, 2nd): Annoyed Baritone
  47. Tenor Trombone 1: Tenor
  48. Tenor Trombone 2: Sad Trombone
  49. Bass Trombone: Bass Trombone
  50. All Euphonium Parts (1st, 2nd): Euphonium
  51. All Tuba Parts (E flat 1, E flat 2, B flat 1, B flat 2): The Original Tuba
  52. Percussion: Flute
  53. Yes, Egoistic Trumpet, you DO have to play 5 parts at once.
  54. If you are playing more than one part at once: pick the part with the melody, or the solo. During the rests, play the other parts.
  55. Flugelhorn has now run out of inspiration. There will only be 56 rules in this list.
  101. If you fail to follow these rules, you will have to sit under the Rain Simulator for 10 minutes (or you could get told off by Flugelhorn).
