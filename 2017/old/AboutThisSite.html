About this site

WARNING: This site contains talking musical instruments and other unrealistic anomalies.

This site is about trombones, if you haven't figured it out already. There are stories about talking musical instruments (arrgh) and the Rad Ant (what the heck) and pictures of trombones too.

A companion site to this is the Awesome Shiny Trombone blog. Stories posted on the blog are sometines added to this site, but sometimes not (with really short ones).



How to use this site (and other site info)

This colour is my favourite colour. (Message to all Americans: colour is spelt colour, not color.) Hex colour code: #f04937. RGB colour code: (240, 73, 55).

The font is a default monospace font with bold. Without bold would look like this: This font has no bold. Hello. Goodbye.

I use bold because without bold, it looks a little weird.

The homepage is called the Sad Trombone Hub, for reasons unknown.

The trombone at the top of this page, when clicked, plays the sad trombone sound effect.

Other clickable images include: the French horn pictures (plays the price is right losing horn effect), pictures of scores (plays whatever music the score shows), and other pictures as indicated.
